Srikanth to meet Sakai in Indonesia Open final

Kidambi Srikanth has defeated No. 1 Son Wan Ho to reach the final of the Indonesia Open, where he will face Kazumasa Sakai.

Th 22-ranked Indian needed three match points but eventually secured a 21-16, 14-21, 24-22 victory over the South Korean.

Earlier on Saturday, Kazumasa Sakai ended HS Prannoy’s impressive run in the competition in the first semi-final.

The Japanese World No. 47 lost the first game to the 25th-ranked Indian, but fought back in the second and third games to earn his place in the final.

Prannoy had reached this stage of the competition by defeating Lee Chong Wei of Malaysia and China’s Chen Long, who are Olympic silver and gold medalists respectively.
