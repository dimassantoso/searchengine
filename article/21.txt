Badminton Superstar Lee Yong Dae To Tie The Knot With An Actress

Korean badminton player Lee Yong Dae is tying the knot with actress Byun Soo Mi, and the couple is expecting a baby in spring!

The two met at a badminton event hosted by Byun Soo Mi’s father. They started dating in 2011 and went public with their relationship in 2012.

Lee Yong Dae expressed, “We’re getting married, and our child will be born soon. I’m very excited about it. I will work harder as an athlete with the support of my family, and I want to do by best to make our marriage an eternal gift for my wife.”

They plan to have a small wedding with only family members attending.

Congratulations to the lovely couple!
