Tony Gunawan (USA)

ony Gunawan has an impressive record as a doubles player and is considered to be one of the all time greats.
He partnered Halim Haryanto to take the honours at the IBF World Championships in 2001.
In 2005 he won the World Championships with Howard Bach, the first time an American had been successful. Tony had move to the States following his 2001 World triumph.
