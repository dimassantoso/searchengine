Badminton great Lee Chong Wei suffers quarter-final upset at China Open

SHANGHAI: Former badminton world number one Lee Chong Wei followed great rival Lin Dan out of the China Open on Friday as he too was on the end of an upset.

The 35-year-old Malaysian Lee, whose world ranking has fallen to seven as he approaches the latter stages of his career, went down in the quarter-finals to Hong Kong's Angus Ng Ka-long in a 70-minute thriller.
