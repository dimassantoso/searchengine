Chinese HK wins mixed doubles at Denmark Open badminton

COPENHAGEN, Oct. 22 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Hong Kong players Tang Chun Man and Tse Ying Suet won the mixed doubles title at the Denmark Open badminton tournament in Odense on Sunday.

The duo, seeded 8th, beat top seed Zheng Siwei/Chen Qingchen of China 24-22, 19-21, 23-21. The Denmark Open is being held from Oct. 17 to 22 in Odense, the third largest city of the country.
