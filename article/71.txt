China wins men's doubles at Denmark Open

Chinese players Liu Cheng and Zhang Nan won the men's doubles title at the ongoing Denmark Open badminton tournament held in Odense, the country's third largest city, on Sunday.

The fifth-seeded duo defeated their Indonesian rivals Marcus Fernaldi Gideon/Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo, seeded No. 2, in three sets 21-16, 22-24, 21-19.
